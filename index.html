Lydia T. Liu

For applications, I am interested in the empirical sciences, and in artificial intelligence, including deep neural networks for unsupervised and reinforcement learning. At Princeton, I am fortunate to work with Amit Singer and Han Liu. I have also spent a wonderful summer at Microsoft Research Cambridge, working with Katja Hofmann and Urun Dogan.
